Sisig Tofu

A meal called Crispy Tofu Sisig is made up of deep-fried tofu cubes, minced onion, and chili peppers. As it contains no meat and no oily components, it is a healthier alternative to typical pig sisig. This recipe for crispy tofu sisig is vegan in addition to being healthful. This indicates that it doesn't include any by-products from animals.

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz. Tofu that is exceptionally firm is cut into tiny cubes.
  • 5 Thai chilies, chopped
  • 2 sliced long green peppers
  • 1 sliced medium yellow onion
  • Vegetable broth, 10 teaspoons
  • Constarch, 1 teaspoon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 table spoon soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups cooking oil
  • To taste, add salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Once the oil gets hot, deep-fry the cubed tofu until it turns crispy. Remove from the pot using a kitchen strainer. Put in a metal colander or into a plate lined with paper towel. Set aside.
  3. Scoop 3 tablesppons of oil from the pot where the tofu were fried. Pour it in a clear pan. Heat the pan on a stove top.
  4. Saute onion, long green peppers and chili.
  5. Add crispy tofu. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  6. Pour the bowl with broth mixture into the pan. Toss the ingredients until coated with the mixture.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with a dipping sauce composed of soy sauce , calamansi, and crushed chili peppers.
