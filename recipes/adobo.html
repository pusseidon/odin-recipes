Adobo

A classic Filipino dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of canola oil
  • 6 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 pc onion, sliced
  • 1 kg chicken cut ups
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/4 cups soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoons of black peppercorns, slightly crushed
  • 2 pcs Knorr Chicken Cubes
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup kale or spinach (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat Oil in pan and saute garlic and onions. Then add chicken to the pan and sear on all sides, until you have a browning in the chicken skin.
  2. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce and water. Add bay leaves, pepper and Knorr Chicken Cubes. Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to simmer, but do not cover the pan. Continue to simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken pieces from sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
  4. Put back fried chicken pieces into sauce. Add sugar and let simmer again for another 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Serve warm.
